Track your progress in National Novel Writing Month.

== Description ==

NaNoWriMo Word Count Tracker is a beta plugin which tracks your progress in NaNoWriMo. Currently it tracks every word in the blog, by every user, this will be changed in future releases.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `NaNoWriMo-tracker.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. You'll now see "Your NaNoWriMo progress: 2229/50,000" in the top right corner of your admin panel.

== Frequently asked questions ==

= What is NaNoWriMo =
NaNoWriMo stands for National Novel Writing Month, which is a writing project where particapants take on the task of writing a novel of at least 50,000 words in the month of November. [See more about it on the projects site.](http://www.nanowrimo.org/)
